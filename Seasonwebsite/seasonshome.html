About Us:

Each season lasts about three months. However, the dates when the seasons begin and end vary depending on whom you ask. Two methods are most commonly used to define the dates of the seasons: the astronomical definition and the meteorological definition.On this website you can learn all about our amazing four seasons and the beauty within them.
